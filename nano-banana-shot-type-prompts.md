# Shot Type Reference Images — Nano Banana Prompts

## Instructions

Generate all 30 images in one Nano Banana session to maintain subject consistency (same woman across all shots).

**Perspective:** Every image is what the VIEWER sees on their phone. You are scrolling TikTok and this is a screenshot from a video. No filming equipment visible. No tripods, ring lights, or camera setups. The viewer's POV.

**Character:** Young Black woman, early-to-mid 20s, natural hair (braids or natural curls), wearing a casual oversized grey hoodie and joggers. Minimal or no makeup. Real person, not a model. Warm brown skin.

**Quality:** Must look like it was filmed on an iPhone rear camera. Slight grain, natural imperfect lighting, not colour-graded. No studio lighting. No professional photography look. The kind of slightly warm, slightly soft image quality you get from an iPhone 14/15 in a real room with real daylight. Think: screenshot from a TikTok you'd actually scroll past.

**Setting:** Her real bedroom or living room. Natural daylight from a window (not even, not perfect). Slightly lived-in background — unmade bed visible, a few items on a shelf, maybe a plant. Not styled, not minimalist, not a set. A real room that a 23-year-old lives in.

**Format:** Vertical 9:16 (1080x1920). No TikTok UI, no text overlays, no watermarks. Just the raw video frame.

**The vibe in one sentence:** A screenshot from a real TikTok video made by a normal person in their bedroom, filmed on their phone.

## How to Generate

1. Open Nano Banana (via Gemini app, AI Studio, or nano-banana.ai)
2. Start with Shot Type prompt 1 to establish the character
3. Generate all 30 shot types in the same session for subject consistency
4. Then generate all 14 visual actions in the same session
5. Use "Thinking" mode for better quality
6. Request 1080x1920 (9:16 vertical) for each
7. Download all 44 images
8. Upload to Cloudinary
9. Map each to its type in the lookup tables
